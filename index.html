About The Perfume

Luxury Butter Perfume is a high-end signature fragrance crafted for elegance and confidence. It opens with a soft creamy vanilla note that immediately gives a warm and luxurious feeling.

The heart of the fragrance develops into smooth buttery accords mixed with subtle floral touches, creating a rich and comforting scent profile that feels both modern and timeless.

As it dries down, the fragrance settles into deep musky and woody notes, leaving a long-lasting trail that stays on the skin for hours.

Top Notes: Vanilla Cream, Soft Sugar Heart Notes: Butter Accord, White Flowers Base Notes: Musk, Amber, Light Woods

This perfume is designed for those who appreciate luxury lifestyle, perfect for daily wear, evenings, and special occasions.
