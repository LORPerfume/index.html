CANCELLATION POLICY

LOR PERFUME • LUXURY FRAGRANCE HOUSE

At LOR Perfume, we aim to provide a smooth and transparent shopping experience. Please review our cancellation terms carefully before placing an order.

1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement, as long as they have not been processed or shipped.

Once an order has been prepared or handed to the delivery carrier, it cannot be cancelled.

2. After Processing

If your order is already being processed or shipped, cancellation is no longer possible due to operational procedures.

3. How to Request Cancellation

To request cancellation, please contact our support team immediately with your order details.

Fast communication increases the chance of successful cancellation.
